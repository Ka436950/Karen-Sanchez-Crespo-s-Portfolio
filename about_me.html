A little about me

I’m someone who stays focused on achieving my goals. I’m diligent in completing the tasks that help me reach those goals, and once I’ve set them, I rarely stray from my path. I’m also very honest about my opinions and will always share my thoughts if asked.

I’m a few months away from completing my Bachelor’s degree in Web Design. Over the past two years, I’ve learned that I always strive for excellence in the projects assigned to me. A great example of this is my capstone project, SignIE—a website we designed to grade ASL signs in real-time, making the language more interactive. In this project, I was responsible for the visual design and the image SignIE presented to the world. By researching our target audience and competition, I created a unique and memorable aesthetic for the brand.

Another thing I’ve discovered about myself is that if there’s a to-do list, I’ll diligently complete every task needed to bring a project to completion. My decisiveness has also been praised in many of the teams I’ve been part of. Whether in school or in my professional life, I always find a way to decide what needs to be done. Thanks to this, I’m able to push projects forward past the ideation stage without dwelling on the what-ifs. I’m also always honest with my opinions and will give them when a client or peer asks. This has earned me the trust of my teammates because they know they can rely on me to be candid.
