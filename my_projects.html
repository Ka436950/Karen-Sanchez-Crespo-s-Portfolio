Resume and Cover Letter

For this project, I focused on tailoring my resume and writing a personalized cover letter for a web design job that matched my skills and goals. I pulled keywords and responsibilities from the job posting to update my resume so it better reflected what they were looking for, especially in areas like design, coding, and teamwork. When writing the cover letter, I made sure to highlight my strengths while still keeping my tone natural and professional, and I included a link to my portfolio so they could see my work. This project really showed me how even small changes to your application materials can make a big difference when it comes to standing out.

Research Proposal

For this project, I proposed a study exploring how color affects the success of marketing and advertising campaigns across four industries: hospitality, restaurants, healthcare, and education. I wanted to better understand why certain colors are used more often and how those choices influence consumer perception and emotional response. I focused on both positive and potentially negative associations, aiming to uncover whether some colors work better in specific contexts. My research proposal includes a literature review, interviews with industry professionals, and a survey using color-based mockups. Participants will help determine which color palettes they find most appealing. I plan to publish my findings and present them at a UCF research conference to share insights with the academic and professional community.
